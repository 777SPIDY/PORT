About

I’m Harish, and I’ve built a solid foundation in Java programming and web development over the past few years. My journey in tech started with courses in programming through platforms like Great Learning and Internshala, where I got hands-on with various web technologies like HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, DBMS, PHP, JavaScript, and React. I’ve also explored cloud computing, focusing on AWS to understand the basics of cloud services.

Academically, I began at St. Joseph’s School in Nellore, where I completed my 10th grade. From there, I moved to Viswasai Junior College, achieving a CGPA of 9.67 during my intermediate years. Later, I pursued a B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences, graduating in 2023. These years of study not only gave me a firm grasp of technical skills but also prepared me for the practical challenges I’d face in the industry.

To get more practical experience, I completed two virtual internships that were pretty pivotal for me. In November 2023, I did a Developer and Technology Job Simulation with Accenture, where I learned about software development methodologies like Waterfall and Agile, and gained some hands-on experience with code debugging. Then, in January 2024, I joined JPMorgan Chase & Co. for a Cybersecurity Job Simulation. There, I worked on analyzing large datasets related to fraud in financial payment services and helped design systems to secure sensitive information. This experience really pushed my understanding of cybersecurity and data analysis.

I’ve also worked on some interesting projects that I’m quite proud of. One such project involved integrating hardware (Raspberry Pi) for environmental monitoring, and another was related to deep learning, where I developed a pneumonia detection system using Python. These projects honed my problem-solving and analytical skills, especially when it came to large datasets and cybersecurity system design.
